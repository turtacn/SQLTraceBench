## Reporting a Vulnerability

Use this section to tell people how to report a vulnerability.

1.  Do not open a GitHub issue if the bug is a security vulnerability.
2.  Send an email to `security@sqltracebench.io`.
3.  We will acknowledge your email within 48 hours.
4.  We will provide a solution or workaround within 7 days.
